frequently asked questions

What types of rooms does Turu offer?

We offer Classic, Deluxe, Executive, and Presidential Suites, each designed to cater to different preferences and needs.

Can I request additional services, such as airport transfers or spa treatments?

Certainly! Our concierge is happy to assist with arranging airport transfers, spa treatments, and any other special requests you may have.

Are there any exclusive benefits for Executive Room guests?

Yes, guests staying in our Executive Rooms enjoy exclusive access to the executive lounge, offering complimentary snacks, beverages, and a tranquil space for work or relaxation.

Can I host events or conferences at Turu?

Certainly! We have versatile event spaces suitable for various occasions. Contact our events team to discuss your specific requirements and make your event memorable.

what are payment methods?

We accept credit and debit cards, cash (IDR), bank transfers, digital wallets, online platforms, traveler's checks, corporate accounts, gift certificates, and offer contactless payment for a convenient experience.
